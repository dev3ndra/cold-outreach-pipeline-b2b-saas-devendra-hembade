# Sources

List of experts referenced in this research, with links, dates, and brief annotations.
Full raw notes (with citation footnotes) are kept in [research/other/cold-email-experts-raw.md](other/cold-email-experts-raw.md).

## AJ Cassata

- **Role:** Co-founder, Revenue Boost (B2B outbound lead-gen agency)
- **Links:** [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ajcassata) · [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@ajcassataofficial) · [Site](https://www.revenueboost.net)
- **Date added:** 2026-07-08
- **Annotation:** Known for the "5P framework" for cold email copy and the "triangle of cold email success" (list building, deliverability, copy). Emphasizes defining ICP and use cases narrowly before writing copy, rather than one generic campaign for everyone.

## Jay Feldman ("Lead Gen Jay")

- **Role:** Founder, Lead Gen Jay
- **Links:** [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@leadgenjay) · [Site](https://leadgenjay.com) · [LinkedIn (company)](https://www.linkedin.com/company/leadgenjay)
- **Date added:** 2026-07-08
- **Annotation:** Scales cold email through large mailbox infrastructure (50–500+ mailboxes) and a "triple tap" framework nested in his triangle of list quality/deliverability/copy. Recommends diversifying providers (Google/Microsoft/private SMTP) past ~500 mailboxes.

## Daniel Fazio ("Cold Email Wizard")

- **Role:** Founder, Cold Email Wizard / Agency U / AI Assisted Agency
- **Links:** [X](https://x.com/blackhatwizardd) · [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/daniel.jf1)
- **Date added:** 2026-07-08
- **Annotation:** Pushes ruthless offer clarity and natural, conversational copy ("write it like you'd say it, then read it out loud"). Practical rule: don't split-test below ~1,000 prospects per variant — under that, send the single best version.

## Nick Abraham

- **Role:** Founder, LeadBird (sends 1–2M cold emails/month)
- **Links:** [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nick-abraham)
- **Date added:** 2026-07-08
- **Annotation:** Systems thinker for cold email infrastructure at scale (domains, DNS, inbox creation, validation pipelines). Key metric: optimize "contact-to-lead ratio" instead of vanity metrics like open rate.

## Guillaume Moubeche

- **Role:** Founder & CEO, lemlist / lempire
- **Links:** [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/guillaume-moubeche-a026541b2)
- **Date added:** 2026-07-08
- **Annotation:** Combines "build in public" content with outbound — a warm-outbound loop where LinkedIn content builds an engaged audience, which is filtered by ICP and targeted with lemlist sequences for much higher reply rates than cold lists.

## Alex Berman

- **Role:** Founder/operator, builds and scales B2B software companies and agencies via cold email
- **Links:** [Site](https://alexberman.com)
- **Date added:** 2026-07-08
- **Annotation:** Advocates niche specificity — one narrow ICP, one strong offer. Known for the "3C's" framework (Compliment, Case study, Call to action) and a minimal 2-email sequence (pitch + one-line bump follow-up).

## Josh Braun

- **Role:** Sales educator/coach
- **Links:** [Example LinkedIn post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/josh-braun_lets-write-a-good-cold-email-using-the-4t-activity-7307052447791796224-AXSH)
- **Date added:** 2026-07-08
- **Annotation:** Creator of the "4T Framework" — Trigger, Think, Third-party Credibility, Talk — designed to make cold emails feel human and curiosity-driven rather than pitchy, ending in a soft CTA.

## Jason Bay

- **Role:** Founder & CEO, Outbound Squad
- **Links:** [Mixmax interview](https://www.mixmax.com/blog/interview-founder-ceo-jason-bay-outbound-squad) · [Example LinkedIn post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jasondbay_i-made-one-hour-of-cold-calls-last-week-activity-7386088660032462848-FeHm)
- **Date added:** 2026-07-08
- **Annotation:** Focuses on multi-channel outbound rhythm and "permission-based openers" (PBO): [context] + [social proof] + [problem], instead of a generic "got a minute?" cold-call opener.

## Xavier Caffrey

- **Role:** Founder, oneaway.io (GTM agency)
- **Links:** [Agency & case studies](https://oneaway.io)
- **Date added:** 2026-07-08
- **Annotation:** Treats cold email as an engineered 7-step system (ICP → Clay waterfall enrichment → multi-domain/inbox infra → 5-line email → 2–3 touch sequence → fast auto-reply triage → ongoing optimization). Recommends 10+ sending domains, 3 inboxes each, warmed 2 weeks, capped at 10–15 sends/inbox/day.

## Maxim Tanevskiy

- **Role:** GTM/outbound operator (solo $0→$1.5M ARR in 9 months)
- **Links:** Active on LinkedIn (no single profile URL captured — see raw notes for referencing posts)
- **Date added:** 2026-07-08
- **Annotation:** Champions "signal-based outbound" — Clay workflows tracking buying signals (job changes, tech installs, hiring spikes) turned into contextual outreach, part of a broader "distribution moat" concept (workflow/partner/trigger capture).
