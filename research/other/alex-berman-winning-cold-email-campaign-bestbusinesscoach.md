## Content

Alex Berman, co-founder and CMO of Experiment 27, discusses cold email marketing strategies for B2B and SaaS companies. The episode covers email fundamentals, campaign structure, and common pitfalls.

**Prerequisites for cold email campaigns:**
- Identify your target audience
- Define your offer clearly
- Prepare case studies or supporting stories

**The Three C's framework:**
- Begin with a compliment
- Include a case study
- Conclude with a call to action

**Effective call-to-action strategy:**
- Manual processes yield higher meeting booking rates
- Frame requests as simple yes/no decisions
- "All marketing works best when you're pointing people to one specific interaction" (paraphrased)

**Best practices:**
- Maintain clean, verified data
- Track open, bounce, reply, and meeting rates
- Test variables individually
- Use .com domains over alternatives
- Avoid sending from primary business domains

**Deal criteria:** Before booking calls, confirm prospects have budget authority, genuine need, and immediate readiness to decide.

**Industry outlook:** As AI automation becomes mainstream, booking rates will decline — starting cold email campaigns now provides a competitive advantage.
