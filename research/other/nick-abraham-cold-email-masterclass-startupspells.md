## Content

Nick Abraham's agency, LeadBird, manages over 150 active clients sending 1–1.5 million cold emails monthly. The masterclass covers five core areas:

**1. Effective offers**
Strong offers must address genuine market pain points rather than generic benefits. For example, video agencies should emphasize lead generation through VSLs, not just quality videos. Including guarantees reduces prospect risk. Abraham advocates maintaining flexibility during sales calls, adjusting pitches when circumstances don't align with original guarantee terms.

**2. Personalization & intent signals**
Three primary intent-detection strategies:
- **LinkedIn engagement scraping:** Identify users who've engaged with competitor content (likes/comments) over recent posts, revealing potential customers
- **Competitor follower targeting:** Scrape followers from competitor LinkedIn pages to reach prospects already interested in the solution category
- **Job posting analysis:** Target companies hiring sales roles (SDR/BDR), indicating emerging sales infrastructure needs

**3. Cold email strategies**
High-volume approach: educational panels create multiple inboxes without monthly costs, though panels may face shutdown risks. Automation handles domain purchasing, DNS configuration, and account creation. Focus optimization on contact-to-lead ratios rather than open/reply rates.

**4. Infrastructure stack**
- **Email providers:** Microsoft and Google inboxes
- **Domain registration:** Porkbun with Cloudflare DNS
- **Sending platform:** Smartlead with provider matching
- **Validation:** MillionVerifier and internal Scrubby tool
- **CRM:** HubSpot (internally recommended Streak for others)

When reply rates drop below 1%, abandon the domain and rotate new inboxes.

**5. Conversion tactics**
- Pre-creating user accounts with login credentials
- Offering free sample outputs (e.g., free YouTube clips) to demonstrate value before signup
- Minimizing friction through no credit card requirements initially
