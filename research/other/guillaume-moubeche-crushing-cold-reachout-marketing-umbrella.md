## Content

Host Itamar Shafir welcomes Guillaume Moubeche, describing him as "an email and social marketing master" who bootstrapped lemlist to $10 million ARR. The episode focuses on effective cold email strategies and campaign structure.

**Background:** Guillaume holds a master's degree in chemical engineering and later pursued marketing studies. After a failed t-shirt business venture with his father, he launched a sales prospecting agency. This experience led him to recognize that existing email tools didn't prioritize personalization. In 2018, he cofounded lemlist with $1,000 and two partners.

**Growth strategy & content marketing:** Guillaume emphasizes "eating your own dog food" — using lemlist internally while building relationships rather than pushing sales. His approach involved:
- Weekly outreach to approximately 100 prospects
- Booking 20–25 meetings per week
- Creating educational content addressing problems identified during conversations
- Building a community to share campaign templates and results

**Cold email fundamentals:** Cold email reaches unknown recipients. Guillaume distinguishes genuine personalization from superficial name-tagging. He stresses that successful outreach means "building relationship with people you don't know."

**Deliverability factors:**
- **Domain authentication:** SPF, DKIM, and DMARC setup establishing domain legitimacy
- **Domain reputation:** Avoiding spam-like behaviors that damage sender credibility
- **Custom tracking domain:** Ensuring personalized tracking separate from shared systems
- **Domain age & volume:** Gradually increasing email volume as reputation builds (warmup phase)

**Lemlist features:**
- Automated warmup clustering users into sending groups
- Intelligent sending algorithms spreading messages over time
- Multi-channel capability integrating LinkedIn and email
- AI-assisted copywriting generating optimized icebreakers

**Real estate campaign example:**
1. **Initial connection:** LinkedIn connection requests
2. **Content phase:** Three-week delay posting relevant industry content
3. **Email sequence:** Personalized messages referencing location and agency expertise
4. **Follow-up strategy:** Alternating LinkedIn and email contact
5. **Interview approach:** Positioning outreach as article research rather than direct sales

**Consultative positioning:** Rather than stating "I'm selling this service," Guillaume advocates emphasizing expertise sharing and relationship-building, particularly when discussing industry pain points with prospects.

**AI integration:** Guillaume discusses forthcoming AI features generating personalized email lines, e.g.: "I just discovered your LinkedIn profile and I saw that you're building Lemlist since 2018, it's impressive to see how you've been able to accelerate growth."

**Multi-channel evolution:** Lemlist expanded from email-only to include LinkedIn messaging and phone call logging within unified campaign sequences, maximizing reach by identifying prospects who haven't responded across multiple channels.

**Future direction:** Guillaume sees AI handling repetitive tasks, allowing humans to focus on relationship-building — the element he considers "most important." He believes AI will eventually prove indistinguishable from human-written copy in this domain.

**Platform capabilities:** The system prevents duplicate outreach by alerting teams when multiple members contact the same prospect, solving coordination issues within sales organizations.
