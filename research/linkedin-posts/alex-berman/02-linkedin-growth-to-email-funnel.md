I gained 1,240 LinkedIn followers in 2 weeks (and woke up my dying account).

When I first decided to crack this, I went through the sales process of one of these "LinkedIn legends."

All the advice boiled down to basic principles: Nail your hooks, tell genuine stories, and use giveaways to drive engagement.

Literally I went through 2 hours of this guy's content (at 3x speed) and didn't hear a SINGLE new thing.

Instead of hiring him, I took the advice and started doing it myself.

Here's how to grow on LinkedIn in 2026:

**1. Pretend You Have No Followers**

When you act like you're starting from scratch, you push yourself to make every word and every idea count. Unfortunately your followers mean nothing.

**2. Spend a Lot More Time Writing**

My average post now takes 3 HOURS from beginning to end. I start with raw ideas captured in a voice note and then spend time writing, editing, and rewriting line by line.

I wrote 3 hooks for the post you're reading:
- "Here's how I grow on LinkedIn"
- "Why you're not stuck in LinkedIn jail and how to break free"
- "I Gained 1,240 LinkedIn Followers in 2 Weeks (and Woke Up My Dying Account)"

The important thing is to try and NOT go with your first instinct without trying more.

**3. Giveaways Are Key**

I pull my best-performing content from my X account or YouTube channel and reframe it as a giveaway.

My "GPT Cold Email Secrets" giveaway received 93 comments in 24 hours, and every single commenter ended up following me.

If a post gets fewer than 10 comments within the first 30 minutes, I delete it and pivot.

This is the fastest way to grow, but isn't the best to get new clients...

For that, you need a FUNNEL: giveaway -> email list -> "buy from me" pitch.

Because of the funnel, around 80% of those followers joined our email list.

In just two weeks, we gained 1,000 net new email subscribers and received 120 net new applications for Galadon Gold.
