I got an AMAZING cold email the other day.

Breakdown for you to steal:

1. Internal-looking subject line (all lowercase too)
2. Super strong offer (cheap + pay on performance)
3. Soft CTA (instead of asking for a call)
4. Further incentive via Amazon gift card

Mark my words: campaigns like this are the antidote to "cold email doesn't work for me".

I would be shocked if the senders here aren't scaling this campaign like crazy right now.

And yes, I will charge my phone.

**Cold email relevance:** concrete structure for subject, offer, CTA, and incentive that you can model and test in your own campaigns.
