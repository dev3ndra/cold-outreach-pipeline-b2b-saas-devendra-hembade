**Topic:** A step-by-step workflow for converting positive cold email replies into booked meetings

---

The second you get a positive reply to a cold email, kick off this workflow to ensure it turns into an actual meeting (we use it to book hundreds/month):

1. Enrich for phone number via LeadMagic. The second a positive reply comes in, we run the lead through waterfall enrichment to find a direct dial. We find one about 40-50% of the time.

2. Call within 5-30 minutes. You want to catch them while your email is still top of mind.

3. Leave a voicemail if no answer. A voicemail ties your call back to the email they already replied to and gives them a reason to call you back.

4. Send 2 follow-up emails if they go quiet. Space them out and reference the original thread so they remember the conversation they started.

5. Make 2-3 more calls if you have the number. One call is never enough. People are busy. You want a yes or a no. Sitting in the middle with no answer is the graveyard.

6. Drop non-responders into a nurture sequence. If they still haven't responded after all of that, put them in a 30-day nurture and re-engage. Don't write them off.

It's not that hard to do, and it's very much worth your time. If you have any other questions on setting it up, let me know.

**Cold email relevance:** highly actionable workflow you can plug into your CRM/sequencer to maximize conversion from "interested" replies.
