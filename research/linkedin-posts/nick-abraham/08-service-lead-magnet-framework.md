**Topic:** Criteria for designing a scalable service-based lead magnet

---

You aren't allowed to complain that "cold email doesn't work" if you haven't tried offering a service lead magnet. Here's how we're generating leads with them:

Quickly, service lead magnets are a free deliverable of sorts that's tied to what you sell.

Ex: We sell B2B lead gen services, so a service lead magnet for us might be a free lead list for prospects.

But, you can't spend hours per lead magnet if you're sending a lot of volume.

So, you need to come up with a service lead magnet that meets the following criteria:

1. Fast to deliver
2. Repeatable to produce
3. Automatable
4. Has a high perceived value

If you meet all of these, that means your prospects will want what you're offering and you can give it to them relatively easily.

And for the "doesn't this just attract freebie seekers crowd?": You have every right to qualify prospects before you send them any assets. You are in the driver's seat.

**Cold email relevance:** gives you a checklist to design scalable, high-perceived-value freebies you can pitch in the email as your main CTA.
