**Topic:** The minimal tech stack for starting a cold email operation from zero

---

If I were in charge of setting up a cold email operation for a company starting from zero with a limited budget, here's the only tech I'd buy:

**INFRA** — Hypertide.io: Easiest and safest way to get infra that actually works without the hassle.

**LIST-BUILDING** — GetLeads: the only unlimited verified B2B contacts database you can use directly in Claude.

**LEAD VERIFICATION** — MillionVerifier / Scrubby: complete email validation, the gold standard for email verification and catch-all verification.

**SEQUENCER** — Smartlead: the sequencer I'd recommend over any other. Great API as well.

**CRM** — Close: the best AI functionality from a CRM that I've seen (fantastic voice agent as well).

You really don't need anything other than this if you want to get cold email up and running.

**Cold email relevance:** gives a lean, battle-tested infra + data + sequencer stack you can replicate when building or optimizing your own outbound system.
