**Topic:** Why traditional lead magnets stopped working and what's replacing them

---

Lead magnets are toast. That free PDF you have? Nobody wants it.

Here's what's replacing it.

Last week I cut almost ALL of our Facebook ads budget....and switched things up

Because I started to see what the trends are, and what's coming....

And I realized that AI isn't just changing the world....

It's changing the marketing world.

It's changing the way people consume content and buy things.

For example, the markets are being flooded with SLOP content:
- PDF guides.
- E-books.
- LinkedIn posts that are CLEARLY AI-generated.
- Blogs (I call them Slop Blogs)
- Videos, images etc

So... with all that AI-generated text and slop being crammed into the internet 24/7....

What kind of COLD prospect is gonna download and read your 47-page E-book or whitepaper?

You sure as hell won't find me reading a 47-page e-book.

But I'm less likely to click or download one, because I'm skeptical that it didn't come from GPT.

So let's talk about how we're re-inventing the lead magnet... and 2 other things I'm doubling down on the next 5 years.

**1. Personal Brand**

People don't trust logos. They trust faces. When everything turns to AI slop, a real human with a real opinion stands out 10x more. I've had a personal brand for 5 years. But now I'm going all in, because this asset is about to be worth more than ever.

**2. Video & YouTube**

Anyone can fake a written post now (over half of long-form LinkedIn posts are already AI). It's a lot harder to fake YOU on camera, sharing real ideas in real time. And long form builds deeper relationships. A post takes seconds to read. But a prospect will watch your video for 20, 30, even 60 minutes… if it's good.

**3. Personalized Lead Magnets [MY SECRET WEAPON]**

Give stuff that actually costs you time. A free audit. A content strategy. A live workshop. People know it's worth money, they know it's high value, and they know it's not some AI-generated junk.

The other day, I consulted with a marketing company that helps landscaping businesses. He asked me if he should build a free industry report as a way to attract new leads and email opt-ins. My response? "Sounds boring lol." Then I told him: DO something for your customers. Offer a free marketing strategy. Or 5 social posts. Or a 1:1 workshop.

AI made it easy for everyone to pump out content. So while your competitors chase 100 new AI tools… zig when they zag.

Personal brand. Video marketing. Personalized lead magnets that actually HELP people and build trust.
