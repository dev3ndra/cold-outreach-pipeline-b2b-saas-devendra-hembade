**Topic:** The real, stacked monthly cost of the typical multi-tool cold email stack

---

Before your next cold email campaign, add up what you're actually paying for tools.

Most people are running four separate subscriptions:
- Apollo / ZoomInfo for leads: $99 to $149/mo
- Clay for enrichment: $149 to $800/mo (climbs fast on volume)
- An email verifier: $50 to $100/mo
- A sequencer like Instantly: $97 to $174/mo

That's $400 to $1,200/month before you've written a single line of copy.

Here's the thing. Cold email didn't get expensive because the channel is expensive. It got expensive because every piece of the workflow was solved by a different company. Leads here. Enrichment there. Verification somewhere else. Nobody put it in one place.

That's the problem we built Consulti to fix. Starting at $97/mo, it combines:
- B2B leads + local business owner emails + creator leads (YouTube, podcasts, Instagram)
- Two-step verification built in
- AI lead qualification + personalized openers
- AI copywriter that pushes straight to your sequencer
- AI reply agent for inbound responses

You'll still need your preferred sequencer (like Instantly or Smartlead), but Consulti replaces several of the other tools most people are paying for separately.

Every feature is on every paid plan. The only thing that changes between tiers is how many leads you can export.

The point isn't "cheaper is better." It's that the hundreds of dollars you're spending stitching multiple tools together could instead go toward actual campaigns, better creative, and more testing.

There's a free tier (1,000 leads/mo) if you want to check the data quality before paying anything.

Run your stack math before your next renewal. The number usually surprises people.
